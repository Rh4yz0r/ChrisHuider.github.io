Terrain Generation

During the third year of my Bachelor in Game Development I researched the subject of terrain generation. At first I wanted to focus on randomly generating terrain, but I shifted focus after realising the process of generating terrain also included some other interesting subjects. This project ended up including work on performance, data storage, in-editor scripting, texture mapping to store data, mesh generation and more.

You can read a lot more about the entire process on wordpress. I describe the process in detail and go into every stuggle I ran into and how I handled it. You can also take a look at the project, which you can find on my github.
