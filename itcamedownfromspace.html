Description

In the first year of my Bachelor at the AUAS I worked on It Came Down From Space.
It Came Down From Space is a game based on "Getting over it" where the player has to traverse a level by grabbing and swinging through the environment. During this project I improved the skills I gained in my previous project (Invasion).

What did I learn?

  • Learned how to do inverse kinematics animation using math.
  • Learned how to manage player input.
  • Improved my 2D math skills.
  • Improved my knowledge on calculating and implementing physics.
  • Improved my object oriented programming skills.
