It Came Down From Space

Description

It came down from space is a game based on "Getting over it" where the player has to traverse a level by grabbing and swinging through the environment. The player had to move their mouse and use both mouse buttons to swing and grab onto the environment and reaching the end. While swinging through the level, the player has to make sure not to bumb into anything, as this will destroy the player's glass protection, which will result in restarting from the start or a save point.

Backstory

This game was made during my second semester in my first year of the Bachelor in Game Development. Our team was free to make whatever game we wanted, the only restriction was that we had to use predetermined assets.

My contribution

During this project I mainly focussed on creating the physics engine for swinging the player. I also worked on player inputs and the alien's inverse kinematics animations.
